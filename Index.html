My favourite Receipe is Fruit Salad

Ingredients required to make Fruit Salad

  • Fruits : Apple, Grapes, Banana, Pomegranate, Watermelon, Papaya
  • a bowl
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Honey
  • Dry fruits

Steps to prepare fruit Salad

  1. Take a bowl
  2. Cut the fruits
  3. Add the fruits in the bowl
  4. Add some milk and mix it well
  5. Add 4 table spoons of sugar
  6. Add some dry fruits
  7. Finally add 2 table spoons of honey and mixt it well and serve it with love.
